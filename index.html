Study Instructions

In this study, you will view pairs of short animated clips showing different people's gestures and body language.

For each pair, you will be asked a question. Please select the person (left or right) who best matches the description.

After each choice, you will be asked how confident you are in your answer (1 = not at all confident, 5 = very confident).

There are no right or wrong answers — we are interested in your natural impressions.

  • Watch each clip carefully before making your choice.
  • You may take as long as you need.
  • Please complete all trials without skipping.

Estimated time: approximately 10–15 minutes.
