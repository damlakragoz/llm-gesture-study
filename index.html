WELCOME

Welcome to the Personality Perception Study!

This research is conducted under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Uğur Güdükbay at Bilkent University.

In this experiment, you will view pairs of short video clips showing different movement profiles.

For each pair, you will be asked to judge which person appears to have MORE of a particular personality trait.

INSTRUCTIONS

First, you will see a fixation cross (+) in the center of the screen. Please focus your eyes on this cross.

You will then see TWO short, silent animations presented side-by-side, along with a personality-related question.

  • Observe both figures as they play for approximately 6 seconds.
  • Read the question at the top of the screen (e.g., "Which person appears more outgoing?").

While or after the videos play, make your selection based on your first impression:

  • Press 1 to select the LEFT video.
  • Press 2 to select the RIGHT video.

After making your choice, you will be asked to rate your confidence in your decision on a scale of 1–5.

There are no right or wrong answers. We are interested in your spontaneous perception.
